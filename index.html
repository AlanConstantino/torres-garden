Our Story

Rooted in Family,
Grown with Love

Torres Garden is a family-run business built on a deep love for plants and a passion for bringing natural beauty into people's lives. What started as a personal hobby grew into something the whole community now enjoys.

Every weekend at the Berryessa Flea Market, we hand-select and care for each plant — from delicate succulents to towering trees — so you can bring home something truly special. We believe everyone deserves a little green in their life.
