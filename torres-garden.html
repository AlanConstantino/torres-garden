Our Story

Rooted in Passion, Grown with Love

Torres Garden is a family-run plant business based at the Berryessa Flea Market in San Jose, California. We specialize in hand-selected succulents, fruit trees, flowering plants, and home decor greenery.

Every plant we sell is carefully grown and handled so it arrives at your home healthy and happy. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, we're here to help you find the perfect plant.
